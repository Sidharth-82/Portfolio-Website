**PyTorch Image Classification Neural Network** is a multi-class image classifier
built in PyTorch to accurately identify images across categories.

## Highlights

- Developed a **multi-class CNN** in PyTorch for image classification.
- Implemented an **animal-face detection** model.
- Leveraged **transfer learning** to enhance lesion detection on leaves.

## What I learned

Transfer learning let a small dataset punch well above its weight — reusing
pretrained features for leaf-lesion detection drastically cut the data and
compute needed to reach usable accuracy. This was an early entry point into the
PyTorch and CV work I now focus on.
