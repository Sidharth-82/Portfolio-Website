**Vex V5 Robotics — Odometry & PID Control** is a **C++** robotic control system
for automated location tracking and field movement of a competition drive train,
built to enable precise, repeatable route planning.

## Highlights

- Implemented **odometry tracking** using kinematic principles to compute
  displacement from strategically placed, freely-rotating tracking wheels.
- Designed and tuned **PID control loops** for acceleration, in-travel error
  correction, and obstacle-related path-deviation handling.
- Integrated odometry with the PID loops for coordinated control over the robot's
  **position and orientation**, enabling precise navigation during autonomous
  rounds.

## What I learned

My first real exposure to closed-loop control and dead-reckoning — the
foundation for the PID, Pure Pursuit, and state-estimation work in my later
robotics projects.
