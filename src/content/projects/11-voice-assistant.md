**AI-Based Voice Assistant** is a Python script that interprets voice commands and
generates spoken responses.

## Highlights

- Interpreted spoken commands and generated replies using the **GPT API**.
- Converted GPT text responses back into speech with a **text-to-speech** API.
- Wired the full voice → text → reasoning → text → voice loop together in Python.

## What I learned

An early, hands-on look at building on top of LLM and speech APIs — chaining
multiple AI services into one conversational loop, well before VLMs became my
main focus.
