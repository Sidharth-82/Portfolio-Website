**MediaPipe Finger Tracking** is a small computer-vision exploration using
Google's **MediaPipe** hand-tracking pipeline.

## Highlights

- Used MediaPipe's finger-tracking algorithm for hand and finger action tracking.
- Explored human-tracking CV algorithms with an eye toward **integration with
  robotics**.

## What I learned

A quick, focused look at landmark-based human tracking and how off-the-shelf CV
pipelines can feed gesture or pose input into a robotics control loop.
