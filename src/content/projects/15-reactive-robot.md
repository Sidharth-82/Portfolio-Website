**Environment Reactive Robot** (MTE 121) is a responsive robot built from Lego
Technic components and programmed in **C** — one of my first engineering builds.

## Highlights

- Equipped the robot with an **IR beacon** (human tracking), an **accelerometer**
  (motion/interaction detection), and a **tactile button** (physical contact).
- Implemented a **state machine** giving the robot six distinct emotional/
  operational states — happiness, anger, excitement, sadness, fatigue, and
  sleepiness.
- Made the robot react dynamically to its environment, human interaction, and
  spatial context.

## What I learned

My introduction to sensor-driven behavior and finite-state design — mapping real
sensor inputs to discrete states is a pattern I've used in every robotics
project since.
