**Mechatronics Engineering, Level 1A–1B — University of Waterloo.** The
foundation year: core math, computing, circuits, and my first co-op. Earned the
**University of Waterloo President's Scholarship** and held **Excellent Standing**
throughout.

**Study terms — courses**

- *Fall 2021 (1A):* Chemistry for Engineers, Linear Algebra, Calculus 1,
  Mechatronics Engineering (MTE 100), Digital Computation (MTE 121).
- *Spring 2022 (1B):* Calculus 2, Structure & Properties of Materials, Statics,
  Circuits (MTE 120), Algorithms & Data Structures (MTE 140).

**Co-op**

- *Winter 2022 — C++ Embedded Software Developer, Peraso (Toronto):* optimized
  read/write routines for pre-production 5G chips, cutting serial command times
  (write 5 ms → 1.3 ms, read 2 ms → 1 ms), and wrote unit tests for command
  conversion.

**Projects & teams**

- Joined the **Midnight Sun Solar Car Team** and began redesigning the
  *pedal control board* — the driver-input board that reads the throttle/brake
  pedals and relays those signals to the car's motor controller.
- Built the *Environment Reactive Robot* (MTE 121) — an autonomous robot that
  senses and responds to its surroundings — and the *Digital Linear Caliper*
  (MTE 202), a measurement instrument with a digital position readout.
